Sweet and sour pork
===================

Ingredients:
------------

Meat:
* 4/3 pound pork
* 6 tablespoons soy sauce
* 1 egg yolk
* 1 tablespoon wheat
* 1 tablespoon farina

Sauce:
* 1/2 red bell pepper
* 1/2 yellow bell pepper
* 1/2 green bell pepper
* 1 tablespoon 9% vinegar
* 1 can pineapple chunks in light syrup (435 gr.)
* 1/2 onion
* 1 teaspoon minced garlic
* 70 gr tomato paste
